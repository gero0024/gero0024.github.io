ABOUT ME

Making art is one of the things I have been doing for long as I can remember. Wether it be comics, character designs, or small sketches, I always was creating the pieces to fit a bigger world. Video games were my main inspiration for this, since when I was younger I would image the characters interacting outside the limits of their world, and that drove me to get the scenes I imagined onto paper with great detail.

Training is another hobby of mine. Martial Arts shows and movies like Dragonball Z and Game of Death inspired me to pick up take classes in Muay Thai and BJJ, which further influenced me to continue training. I try to make exercise a habit I'll take with me in the long term, since Bruce Lee once said "Long-term consistency trumps short-term intensity." Bruce Lee
