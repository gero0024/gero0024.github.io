Tag list

Images
The Images (img) tag allows you to source an image from your repositories and display on a webpage. It needs two pieces of information to work. The Source (src) part provides a direct postion of the image. The (alt) part provides alternative describing the image.
Anchor
The Anchor (a) tag allows you to link different websites to your own. You can add an alternatve titles with this tag, so the links looks more appealing and are more descriptive.
